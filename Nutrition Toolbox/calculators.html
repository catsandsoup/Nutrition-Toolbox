PAL Value Guide:

  • 1.2 - 1.3: Sedentary (little to no exercise)

  • 1.6 - 1.7: Moderately active (moderate exercise 3-5 days/week)
  • 1.8 - 1.9: Very active (hard exercise 6-7 days/week)
  • 2.0 - 2.4: Extremely active (very hard exercise, physical job, training)
